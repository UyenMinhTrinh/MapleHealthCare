Employment

Join Maple Health Care’s caring team. We provide flexible schedules, supportive leadership, and meaningful work that helps clients stay independent at home. Competitive pay, steady hours, and growth opportunities for the right candidates.

  • Personal Support Workers / Community Health Workers

    Support clients with daily living: bathing, meal prep, light housekeeping, mobility, and companionship. Ideal for compassionate caregivers who value patient-first care.

    • PSW/CHW certificate or equivalent experience
    • Evenings/weekends availability an asset
    • Driver’s license and vehicle are a plus

  • Personal Support Workers with Vehicle

    Serve clients across Ottawa with reliable transportation. Mileage or travel time may be available depending on assignment and route.

    • Valid driver’s license + insured vehicle
    • Comfortable traveling between client homes
    • PSW/CHW certificate preferred

  • Live-in Caregivers

    Provide continuous, compassionate care in the client’s home. Rotational schedules available to share weekend coverage with coworkers.

    • Full-time, part-time, and temporary roles
    • Experience with seniors & medication reminders preferred
    • Must be comfortable with shared weekend rotation

  • Registered Nurses / RPNs

    Provide one-on-one nursing in home or facility settings: wound care, medication administration, monitoring, documentation, and client education.

    • Active CNO registration required
    • Home care or LTC experience an asset
    • Shifts available for casual and part-time nurses

How to Apply

Email your resume to maple4care@gmail.com with the role in the subject line. In your email, include your availability, areas you can work, certifications, and whether you have a vehicle.

Maple Health Care is an equal opportunity employer. We thank all applicants for their interest; only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
