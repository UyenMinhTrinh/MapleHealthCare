Employment Opportunities

Join the Maple HealthCare team and help us provide compassionate, professional care in the Ottawa area.

Personal Support Workers

We are currently looking for Community Health Workers, Resident Care Attendants, or foreign-trained nurses; short-term and long-term positions available in the Ottawa area to assist seniors living in their own homes.

Apply: Send applications directly to maple4care@gmail.com

Personal Support Workers with Vehicle

We are always looking for Personal Support Workers who drive and have their own vehicles.

Live-in Caregivers

Full-time, part-time, and temporary positions available. Must be willing to share weekend coverage with co-workers (Example: one works Sunday–Monday–Tuesday and the other works Wednesday–Thursday–Friday–Saturday). Driver’s license and own vehicle an asset, but not necessary.

Registered Nurses / LPNs

We can always use a few more RNs and LPNs looking to pick up extra shifts on their days off. Ideal for nurses who enjoy one-on-one bedside nursing or wish to keep their skills current in care facilities such as retirement homes or long-term care.

Student Nurses

We love hiring Student Nurses every summer. Our team benefits from enthusiastic new staff to help cover vacations for regular staff, and students benefit by gaining invaluable hands-on nursing experience and confidence. Must be willing to accept both shorter and longer shifts.
